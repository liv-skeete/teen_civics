Why Civic Education Matters

Civic education helps you understand how decisions are made in our democracy and how you can participate. By learning about government and current issues, you become better prepared to:

  • Make informed decisions when you vote
  • Understand how laws affect your daily life
  • Participate in community discussions
  • Advocate for issues you care about
  • Become an active, engaged citizen

Remember: You don't have to wait until you're 18 to start learning about government and making a difference!
